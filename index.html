About Us

Big Rigs Clan is a SIG of the CMCA. We are a community of owners and builders of large motorhomes, coaches and converted rigs who share a passion for travel, craftsmanship and the unique lifestyle that comes with life on the road. We are a social club focused on friendship, supporting each other’s projects, and creating opportunities to meet through rallies (annual BRC gatherings), social media and relaxed get‑togethers across Australia.

Join Us

Interested in becoming a financial member of Big Rigs Clan? We'd love to have you.

Membership Includes:
• Access to club rallies and events
• Member support network
• Club updates and newsletters
• Voting rights at our AGM held Easter each year.

Annual Membership Fee : Just $5 per year per Big Rig with a once off joining fee of $30.

Vehicle Eligibility:
To become a financial member, Big Rig vehicles must conform to one of the following configurations:
• Converted Bus or Coach over 10 mtrs that is fully self‑contained.
• Converted Truck or Prime Mover with trailer OR 5th Wheeler that is fully self‑contained.

You MUST either own a converted bus/big rig or be in the process of converting a bus/big rig to be considered for membership of The Big Rigs Clan.
